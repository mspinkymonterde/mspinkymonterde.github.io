Manual for working with me

633 words • 3 min to read

Hi, I’m Pinky, a third-year IT student with a passion for blending creativity and technology. I’m constantly learning and growing, aiming to make a meaningful impact through my projects and collaborations.

Note: This manual is designed to help you understand how I work best. I hope it serves as a guide to foster collaboration and productivity. I’d love to learn about your work preferences, too!

The basics you should know about me

I’m curious, detail-oriented, and driven by a strong work ethic. I enjoy breaking down complex problems into manageable solutions, and I’m always eager to learn and improve. I value collaboration but also thrive in independent tasks that allow me to explore my creative side.

My best working patterns look like

I’m most productive in the mornings or late afternoons when I can work uninterrupted in a quiet space. I like to plan my tasks ahead, often using digital tools to organize and track my progress. While I value structure, I also adapt well to changing priorities when needed.

My absolute requirements to do great work are...

  • A clear understanding of project goals and expectations
  • Access to the right tools and resources
  • A quiet and organized workspace
  • Open communication with team members or mentors for guidance and feedback

The best way to give me feedback is...

I prefer feedback that is clear, specific, and constructive. A private conversation or written notes work best for me, as they give me time to process and reflect. I appreciate when feedback includes actionable suggestions I can immediately apply.

When I’m dealing with stress...

I take a short break to clear my mind, often by sketching, taking photos, or organizing my space. When stress levels are high, I find it helpful to talk things out with someone I trust or create a prioritized to-do list to regain focus.

Beyond work, I’m really passionate about...

I love exploring photography, graphic design, and interior styling, as these allow me to express my creativity. I’m also deeply interested in technology’s potential to improve daily life, and I enjoy learning new tools or techniques that inspire innovation.

How I like to celebrate successes

I celebrate small wins with personal rewards, like treating myself to my favorite food or taking time to relax. For big milestones, I enjoy sharing the moment with friends or family.

The values I prioritize in work or life

Integrity, collaboration, and continuous growth are my core values. I believe in being honest, dependable, and always striving to improve both personally and professionally.

My long-term goals include...

I aim to become a versatile IT professional with expertise in software development and UI/UX design. Eventually, I hope to lead meaningful projects that have a real-world impact, fostering creativity and innovation.

How I handle challenges or mistakes

I see challenges and mistakes as opportunities to learn and grow. When faced with a setback, I analyze what went wrong, seek feedback or advice if needed, and focus on finding solutions rather than dwelling on the problem.
